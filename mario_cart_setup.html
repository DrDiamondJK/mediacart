Step 9

Recording Images and Video

  • Deploy the attached footswitch to the field for the provider to use.
  • The footswitch is preferable to the touchscreen controls.
  • The provider will NOT be able to take images or video with the on-camera controls.
  • The provider will NOT be able to take images or video with the footswitch attached to the boom.
  • The nCare unit will display thumbnails of recorded video clips and images as they are recorded during the procedure.

Step 10

Printing Images

When ready, click
Print icon
(media/image24.png)
in the lower right corner of the screen.
  • Choose the destination printer. The default printer is Liberty OR1. If you need to change the printer to Liberty OR4, click the dropdown box to select a different printer.
  • Please only select Liberty Printers
